My Travel Stories

Goa Beach Trip

Goa is one of India's most famous beach destinations. Known for its golden beaches, vibrant nightlife and Portuguese heritage, Goa offers a relaxing yet exciting experience. During my visit I enjoyed watching the sunset at Baga Beach and tasting delicious seafood by the sea.

Manali Snow Adventure

Manali is a beautiful hill station located in Himachal Pradesh surrounded by snow-covered mountains and pine forests. It is famous for adventure activities like trekking, skiing and paragliding. My trip to Solang Valley was unforgettable with breathtaking views and cool mountain air.

Jaipur Heritage Tour

Jaipur, known as the Pink City of India, is rich in royal history and architecture. Famous attractions include Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort and City Palace. Walking through the colorful markets and tasting traditional Rajasthani food made this trip a wonderful cultural experience.
